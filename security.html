🔒 Security

Security Policy

Your security is our top priority at NETLINK AGENCIES

🔐 Account Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your account and personal information including Firebase Authentication with encrypted passwords.

💡
Use a strong, unique password for your NETLINK AGENCIES account. Never share your password with anyone — including NETLINK AGENCIES staff.
  • All passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Email verification is required for account access
  • Suspicious login attempts are automatically blocked
  • You can reset your password anytime via email

💳 Payment Security

All payment transactions are processed through secure, verified payment gateways including PayHero and PayPal.

  • M-Pesa transactions use Safaricom's secure STK Push system
  • PayPal payments use PayPal's SSL encryption
  • We never store your M-Pesa PIN or PayPal password
  • All withdrawal requests are manually verified by admin

🛡️ Data Protection

Your personal data is stored securely on Google Firebase — one of the world's most trusted cloud platforms.

  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • You can request deletion of your account at any time
  • We only collect data necessary to operate the platform

⚠️ Protect Yourself

🚨
NETLINK AGENCIES will NEVER ask for your password, M-Pesa PIN, or full payment card details via WhatsApp, email or phone call.
🔗
Always verify you are on the official site: netlinkagencies.linkpc.net before entering any login details.
📱
If you suspect your account has been compromised, reset your password immediately and contact us at info@netlinkagencies.co.ke

📧 Report a Security Issue

If you discover a security vulnerability or suspicious activity, please report it immediately to:

info@netlinkagencies.co.ke

We take all security reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
