2️⃣ How to Register

Registration on NETLINK AGENCIES is completely FREE. Anyone above 18 years old with a valid email address and phone number can join.

Step-by-Step Registration:

  1. Visit netlinkagencies.linkpc.net/register
  2. Enter your full name
  3. Choose a unique username (lowercase letters and numbers only, no spaces)
  4. Enter your email address (you'll receive notifications here)
  5. Enter your phone number (M-Pesa number for Kenya)
  6. Select your country from the dropdown list
  7. If someone referred you, enter their referral code
  8. Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
  9. Click Register
⚠️ Important: Use a valid email address you have access to. All important notifications including activation confirmation, withdrawal updates, and account changes are sent to this email.

After Registration:

After registering you will be redirected to the activation page. Registration only creates your account — you must activate it to access earning features.
